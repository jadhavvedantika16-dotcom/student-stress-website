Understanding Student Stress

Student stress is more than just exam fear. It can come from expectations, competition, family pressure, and social comparison.

Why It Happens?

High academic expectations, fear of judgment, and uncertainty about the future.

Why It Matters?

Ignoring stress can affect mental health, focus, and overall well-being.

What We Can Do?

Awareness, communication, and healthy habits can reduce its impact.
